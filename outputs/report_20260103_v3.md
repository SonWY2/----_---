## WHY SMALL-CAP PDUFA RUN-UPS ARE HARDER TO CAPTURE

### 1. **Information Lag**
- Small-caps don't get broad analyst coverage
- BLA/NDA filings often buried in 8-Ks or earnings calls
- PDUFA calendars updated 2-4 weeks AFTER filing acceptance
- By time retail investors find them, entry windows passed

### 2. **Institutional Front-Running**
- Hedge funds monitoring SEC filings directly catch NDA acceptances same-day
- Institutions buy during filing acceptance news (before PDUFA date set)
- Retail investors discover via calendars 4-8 weeks later

### 3. **Higher Volatility = Wider Spreads**
- Small-cap biotechs often trade with 3-8% bid-ask spreads
- Slippage on entry/exit can eat 10-15% of gains
- Market makers widen spreads during Run-ups

### 4. **Liquidity Risk**
- Average daily volume <500K shares for many small-caps
- Large positions (>$50K) hard to exit cleanly
- Stop-losses can trigger cascades in thin markets
